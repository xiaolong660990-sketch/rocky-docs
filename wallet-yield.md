# Wallet Yield

Wallet Yield lets you put idle capital to work without leaving Rocky. Alongside perpetual trading, it's the second pillar of Phase 1 and a core entry point into Canton's liquidity layer.

## How it works

Deposit stablecoins and earn yield from strategies powered by Rocky's own trading activity:

* **Delta-neutral funding-rate strategies** — capture funding payments without taking directional exposure.
* **Trading-fee yield** — a share of the fees the exchange generates.
* **Canton ecosystem rewards** — Canton Coin (CC) incentives.

## Target yield

**5–8%** — illustrative, depending on market conditions. Yield is variable and not guaranteed.
