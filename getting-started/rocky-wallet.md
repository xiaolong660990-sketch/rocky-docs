# Rocky Wallet

Rocky Wallet is where your assets, addresses, and signing approvals live.

You can either:

* **Connect an existing Canton wallet** (Cantonloop, Console), or
* **Sign up with email**, and Rocky Wallet generates a self-custodial wallet for you.

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Back up your recovery phrase.** Your email logs you in — it cannot recover your wallet. Only your recovery phrase can. Write it down offline before you deposit or trade.
{% endhint %}

## Custody model

* Keys are held locally and unlocked with your password.
* Rocky never accesses your private key.
* Sending assets, signing messages, and accepting offers all require your confirmation.
