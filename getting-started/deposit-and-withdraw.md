# Deposit & Withdraw

Deposit supported Canton assets to your Rocky address; withdrawals return funds to your own wallet on-chain.

## Deposit

1. Open **Receive** and copy your address (or show the QR code).
2. Send only Canton-supported assets to this address.

## Withdraw

1. Open **Send**, enter the recipient and amount.
2. Review the network fee and estimated total.
3. Confirm.

{% hint style="danger" %}
**Transfers cannot be reversed.** Double-check the recipient, amount, and network before confirming.
{% endhint %}
