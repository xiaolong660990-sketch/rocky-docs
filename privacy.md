# Privacy

Rocky's core promise: **no one can see your positions.**

On a transparent perp DEX, anyone can read your size, entry, and liquidation price — and trade against them. On Rocky, that information is confidential, while the protocol's overall solvency remains provable.

## What's private vs. verifiable

| Private to you | Publicly verifiable |
| --- | --- |
| Positions & size | The protocol is fully collateralized |
| Balances | Aggregate open interest |
| Liquidation levels | Matching and settlement followed the rules |

## Why it matters

* **No liquidation hunting.** Large traders aren't targeted because their stops and liquidation levels aren't visible.
* **No front-running your intent.** You can build a position without telegraphing it to the market.
* **Institutions can participate.** Confidentiality is a requirement for many professional participants.

{% hint style="info" %}
Privacy for users, transparency for the protocol — you keep your edge, and the exchange still proves it's fully backed.
{% endhint %}
