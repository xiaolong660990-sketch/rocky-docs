# Privacy

Rocky's core promise: **no one can see your positions.**

On a transparent perp DEX, anyone can read your size, entry, and liquidation price — and trade against them. On Rocky, that information is confidential.

| Private to you | Publicly verifiable |
| --- | --- |
| Positions & size | The protocol is fully collateralized |
| Balances | Aggregate open interest |
| Liquidation levels | Matching followed the rules |

{% hint style="info" %}
**Why it matters** — whales stop getting their liquidations hunted, and everyone trades without telegraphing intent. Privacy for users, provable solvency for the protocol.
{% endhint %}
