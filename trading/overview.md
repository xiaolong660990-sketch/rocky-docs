# Trading Overview

Rocky offers **perpetual futures** — leveraged contracts with no expiry — on crypto and tokenized real-world assets.

## Key concepts

* **Long / Short** — profit when the price rises (long) or falls (short).
* **Leverage** — control a larger position with less margin. Amplifies both gains and losses.
* **Margin modes** — **cross** (positions share collateral) or **isolated** (margin capped per position).
* **Mark price** — a manipulation-resistant reference price used for PnL and liquidations.
* **Funding rate** — periodic payments between longs and shorts that keep the perpetual near the underlying price.
