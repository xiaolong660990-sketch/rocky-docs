# Order Types

* **Market** — executes immediately at the best available price. Use when you want certainty of execution.
* **Limit** — rests on the order book at your chosen price. Use when you want to control your entry or exit price.
* **Stop** — triggers a market or limit order when the price reaches your stop level. Commonly used as a stop-loss.
* **Take-Profit** — triggers an order when the price reaches your target. Locks in gains automatically.
* **TWAP** — splits a large order into smaller slices executed over time, reducing market impact.

{% hint style="info" %}
You can attach a **take-profit** and **stop-loss** when opening a position, so your exits are set from the start.
{% endhint %}
