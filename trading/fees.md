# Fees

Fees are charged per fill and scale with your role and 30-day volume. Network costs are abstracted — you pay a single trading fee, not per-click gas.

| Role | Fee | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Maker | 0.010% | Adds liquidity to the book |
| Taker | 0.035% | Removes liquidity |
| High volume | lower | Fees decrease as 30-day volume grows |

{% hint style="info" %}
Figures are examples for layout; the final fee schedule will be confirmed before launch.
{% endhint %}
